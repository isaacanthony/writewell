The Mission:

The mission of Write Well is to steer you through cliche-ridden and clunky sentences into clear, powerful, attention-grabbing language that reveals and markets the best of you.





Your Guide:

Iris Krasnow is a seasoned journalist, writing professor and New York Times bestselling author who coaches private clients in all things that require the written word. She can help you with a range of documents ― anything that demands that you come off as a focused and capable STAR on the printed page.
